Stack CAS Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-07

Stack CAS is an offline calculator app for Android.

Stack CAS does not collect, transmit, share, or sell personal data.

Stack CAS does not require an account, does not use analytics or ads, and does not request the Android INTERNET permission.

To restore your previous session, the app stores the following information only on your device:

  • calculator stack state
  • undo history
  • user-defined variables
  • display mode and small UI preferences

This information stays on your device and is not sent off the device.

For privacy questions, email stackcas@neravo.cc.
